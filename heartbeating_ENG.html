The "Heatbeating" exhibition, 10.03.2016-20.04.2016

Quoting the announcement at the exhibition hall of Konstantinovsky Palace:

"In the framework of a project "Modern Russian artists", we are happy to open the exhibition of Anna Zheleznyakova, a young painter from Saint-Petersburg who graduated from famous Academy of Arts (named in honor of I.E.Repin). Anna specializes in graphics, namely, in book illustrations and easel graphics, takes part in cinema and theater projects. Her artwoks are so versatile that they seem to be created by different artists. The present exhibition combines book illustrations to Rostan's "Cyrano de Bergerac" and a series of work "Dreaming of Greece". It is symbolic that the exhibition takes place during the year of cultural exchange between Russia and Greece. One can visit the exhibition until the 20th of April. "
